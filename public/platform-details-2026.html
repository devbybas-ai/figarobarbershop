Executive Summary

The Figaro Barbershop platform is a custom-built, all-in-one digital solution designed specifically for Figaro Barbershop Leucadia. It replaces fragmented tools -- third-party booking platforms with recurring fees, disconnected systems, and an outdated website -- with a single, branded system that the shop owns completely.

This platform delivers a premium public-facing website that attracts and converts new clients, paired with a powerful internal administration dashboard that streamlines daily operations for the owner, barbers, and front desk staff.

Public Website

A branded, high-end website that establishes Figaro's digital presence, attracts walk-ins, and converts visitors into booked appointments.

Live

Premium Homepage

A fully designed homepage featuring real shop photography, smooth scroll animations, customer testimonials, an Instagram feed integration, and prominent calls-to-action. Optimized for both desktop and mobile with a responsive layout built around the Figaro brand identity.

Problem solved: Replaces a basic or outdated web presence with a premium, branded experience that reflects the quality of the shop and builds trust with potential clients before they walk in the door.

Barber Directory & Profiles

A "Meet the Crew" page showcasing each barber with their photo, title, specialties, and personal bio. Each barber has a dedicated profile page with a portfolio gallery of their work and a direct link to book with them.

Problem solved: Clients often choose a barbershop based on the barbers. Individual profiles let each barber build a personal brand under the Figaro umbrella, helping attract clients who want a specific style or expertise.

Online Booking System

A multi-step booking wizard that guides clients through selecting services, choosing a barber, picking an available date and time slot, and confirming their appointment. The availability engine checks each barber's real schedule and existing appointments to only show open slots.

Problem solved: Eliminates missed bookings from phone tag and DMs. Clients can self-serve 24/7, and the shop fills chairs without staff spending time on the phone. Real-time availability prevents double-booking.

Live

New Client Intake Form

A digital intake form for new clients capturing contact information, preferred barber, and notes. Includes a kiosk mode that auto-resets after 8 seconds for iPad use at the front desk. Intake links can be emailed to clients in advance.

Problem solved: Replaces paper clipboards and manual data entry. Client information is captured digitally, saved to the database instantly, and linked to their profile for every future visit. No more lost intake sheets or illegible handwriting.

Administration Dashboard

The internal command center where the owner, barbers, and front desk staff manage daily operations -- from the day's appointments to revenue tracking.

Live

Owner Dashboard Overview

A real-time overview showing today's appointment count, walk-ins received, revenue generated, active barbers on shift, and total registered clients. Designed to give the owner an instant pulse on the business the moment they log in.

Problem solved: The owner no longer needs to ask "how'd we do today?" or check multiple sources. One screen shows exactly how the business is performing right now.
Live

Appointment Management

A day-view appointment board with date navigation showing all appointments across all barbers. Appointments can be filtered by status (Scheduled, In Progress, Completed, Cancelled, No-Show) and updated in real-time as clients arrive and services are completed.

Problem solved: Replaces wall calendars, whiteboards, and mental tracking. The entire team sees the same schedule, reducing confusion about who's next, who's running behind, and which chair is open.
Live

Front Desk Register

A complete front-desk workflow in one screen: a live queue showing today's clients and their status (Waiting, In Chair, Done, Paid), a walk-in check-in form with client selection and barber tap-to-assign, multi-service selection, checkout with cash/card payment processing and tip entry, and a QR code generator for the self-service kiosk intake form.

Problem solved: Replaces the front desk juggling act of sticky notes, memory, and shouting across the shop. Walk-ins are logged instantly, the queue is visible to everyone, and checkout is fast and tracked.
Live

Client Management

A searchable client database showing all registered clients with their intake completion status, contact info, preferred barber, visit history, and total spend. Includes the ability to send intake forms via email, view client details, and soft-delete clients (preserving data integrity while removing them from active views).

Problem solved: Builds a real client database the shop owns -- not locked in a third-party app. Enables personalized service ("Hey Marcus, same cut as last time?"), targeted marketing, and business insights on client retention.
Live

Service Management

Full create, edit, and delete capability for the service menu. The owner can add new services, update pricing and descriptions, set durations, assign categories, and deactivate services that are no longer offered -- all without needing a developer.

Problem solved: Price changes and new services go live immediately without waiting for a website update. The public menu and internal booking system always reflect the current offerings.
Live

Barber Management

The owner can manage barber profiles including their display name, title, commission rate, tagline, specialties, and whether they operate as a commission barber or booth renter. This controls what appears on the public website and how their earnings are calculated.

Problem solved: Supports both business models under one roof -- commission barbers and booth renters. Commission splits are calculated automatically, and barber profiles stay current without manual website updates.
Live

Inventory & Product Management

Product catalog with stock tracking, reorder thresholds, and quick-restock actions. The owner can add products with pricing, cost, and SKU, monitor stock levels at a glance, and delete products that are no longer carried.

Problem solved: No more running out of pomade mid-week or guessing what needs to be reordered. Stock levels are tracked in real-time, and reorder alerts prevent stockouts that mean lost product sales.
Live

Analytics Dashboard

A real-time analytics suite with week and month views, period-over-period comparison, and four key metrics: gross revenue, shop cut, commission owed, and total tips. Includes a revenue-by-day bar chart, service popularity breakdown (pie chart), and a top barbers leaderboard.

Problem solved: Transforms gut feelings into data-driven decisions. The owner can see which services are most popular, which days are busiest, and which barbers are driving the most revenue -- enabling smarter scheduling, pricing, and staffing.
Live

Revenue Reporting (Owner Only)

A detailed revenue breakdown accessible only to the owner, showing gross revenue, the shop's cut, total commissions owed, tips collected, booth rent income, payment method distribution, and per-barber earnings in a sortable table.

Problem solved: Replaces end-of-week envelope math and spreadsheets. The owner knows exactly what the shop earned, what each barber is owed, and how clients are paying -- making payouts accurate and bookkeeping straightforward.
Live

Payment Processing & Tracking

Records every payment with automatic commission calculation based on each barber's rate. Supports 8 payment methods (Cash, Card, Stripe, Zelle, CashApp, Square, Venmo, Other). Automatically splits each transaction into shop cut and barber cut, with separate tip tracking. Each barber's profile stores their personal Zelle and Venmo handles, so the team can receive direct payments and tips through the methods they actually use.

Problem solved: Eliminates manual commission math and end-of-day reconciliation. Every dollar is tracked, split correctly, and attributed to the right barber -- regardless of how the client pays. Barbers get paid through their preferred method (Zelle, Venmo, CashApp) without the owner manually tracking handles.

Problem solved: Gives barbers ownership of their performance data without exposing the full shop's financials. Motivates accountability and lets barbers track their own earnings without asking the owner.
Live

Barber Calendar

A day-view calendar overlaid with the barber's set working hours, showing their booked appointments as visual cards. Barbers can see gaps in their schedule and understand their day's flow at a glance.

Problem solved: Barbers know exactly when their next client is, how long they have between appointments, and when their day ends -- without checking a shared calendar or asking the front desk.

Barber Transaction History

A complete record of each barber's financial transactions including service payments, commissions earned, tips received, booth rent charges, and any manual adjustments. Filterable and sortable.

Problem solved: Full financial transparency for every barber. No more disputes about what was earned or owed -- every transaction is logged, timestamped, and categorized.

Barbers can update their own profile photo, tagline, and specialties. Changes flow directly to the public website. Photo uploads are validated for format and size (max 5MB).

Problem solved: Booth renters operate semi-independently. This tool lets them manage their own payment setup, track their business within Figaro's system, and get paid directly through the method they prefer -- without complicating the shop's commission-based accounting.

Security, Authentication & Infrastructure

Enterprise-grade security and access controls protecting the shop's data, clients' personal information, and financial records.

Live

Role-Based Access Control

Four distinct user roles (Owner, Barber, Receptionist, Staff) with granular permissions controlling what each person can see and do. The navigation, available pages, and API access all adapt based on the logged-in user's role.

Problem solved: The owner sees revenue and can manage everything. Barbers see only their own data. The receptionist handles the front desk. No one accesses more than they should -- protecting sensitive financial data and client information.
Live

Secure Authentication

Industry-standard authentication with bcrypt password hashing (12 rounds), JWT session tokens, CSRF protection, and rate-limited login attempts (5 per minute per IP) to prevent brute-force attacks.

Problem solved: Protects staff accounts and client data from unauthorized access. Rate limiting prevents automated attacks, and strong encryption ensures passwords can't be recovered even if the database were compromised.
Live

Security Headers & HTTPS

Full suite of security headers (Content Security Policy, HSTS, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options) enforcing encrypted connections, preventing clickjacking, and blocking common web attacks.

Problem solved: Ensures the website meets modern security standards, protects client data in transit, and prevents the site from being embedded or manipulated by malicious third parties.
Live

Data Privacy & Soft Deletes

Client records use soft-delete (marking as inactive rather than destroying data), preserving referential integrity for appointment and payment history. Rate limiting on public forms (10 submissions per minute) prevents spam and abuse.

Problem solved: Balances data privacy with business needs. A deleted client's past appointments and payments remain intact for accounting, while their profile is removed from active views -- following data protection best practices.
Live

Dedicated Hosting Infrastructure

The platform runs on a dedicated VPS (Virtual Private Server) with Nginx, PM2 process management, and PostgreSQL -- giving Figaro full control over its data and operations. Hosting costs just a couple hundred dollars per year (paid monthly or annually), plus a standard annual domain renewal -- the same domain cost as the current site. No per-transaction fees, no per-booking commissions, and no platform lock-in.

Problem solved: Hosting is a flat, predictable cost of around $175 per year -- plus the same domain renewal the shop already pays. No per-transaction fees, no per-booking commissions, and all data stays on Figaro's own server.

Future Phases

The platform's database foundation is already built to support these upcoming features. Each phase adds new revenue streams, client engagement tools, and operational capabilities.

Phase 2 -- Client Portal

Give clients their own login to manage appointments, view history, and complete intake forms -- turning one-time visitors into engaged regulars.

  • Client authentication with email login and password reset
  • Personal appointment history and upcoming bookings
  • Online rebooking from past appointments
  • Intake form completion and profile management

Phase 3 -- Loyalty Program

Reward returning clients with a points-based loyalty system that drives repeat visits and increases lifetime client value.

  • Points earned automatically on every service
  • Tiered membership levels (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum) with escalating perks
  • Rewards catalog -- redeem points for discounts, free services, or products
  • Admin dashboard for managing tiers, rewards, and tracking member activity

Phase 4 -- Memberships & Subscriptions

Predictable recurring revenue through subscription plans that lock in regular clients and guarantee monthly income.

  • Configurable membership plans (weekly or monthly billing)
  • Stripe-powered automatic payment collection
  • Included visit tracking and plan enforcement
  • Member management with pause, cancel, and upgrade capabilities

Phase 5 -- Marketing Center & Client Automations

Targeted outreach tools and automated client engagement -- from thanking clients after a visit to winning back lapsed regulars with personalized offers.

  • Email campaign builder with reusable templates
  • SMS campaign support for time-sensitive promotions
  • Audience segmentation (by barber, service history, visit frequency)
  • Campaign scheduling with delivery, open, and click tracking
  • Automated "Thank You" message sent after each visit -- keeps the shop top-of-mind and builds loyalty
  • Win-back automation: clients who haven't visited in 2 months automatically receive a "$10 off your next service" offer to bring them back

Phase 6 -- Product Pickup Orders

Let clients order products online and pick them up at the shop -- turning inventory into an additional revenue stream beyond service appointments.

  • Online product ordering from the public website with real-time stock availability
  • Order tracking: which client ordered what, order status, and estimated pickup time
  • Dashboard view for staff to see pending pickups and mark orders as collected
  • Notification to the client when their order is ready for pickup

Phase 7 -- House Calls & Wedding Packages

Expand Figaro's reach beyond the shop walls with premium on-location services -- from house calls to full wedding day grooming packages for the groom and groomsmen.

  • House call booking with travel fee ($150+) and location details
  • Wedding packages: groom, groomsmen, and wedding party grooming bundles
  • Dedicated "Wedding Services" page on the public site, optimized for "wedding barbers" and "local wedding barbershop" search terms
  • Custom pricing and scheduling for on-location appointments outside regular shop hours
  • Deposit collection at booking for premium on-location services

Stripe Payment Integration

Accept credit card payments directly through the platform with Stripe, enabling online payment at booking and in-shop card processing.

  • Online payment at time of booking (deposit or full payment)
  • In-dashboard card processing for walk-in payments
  • Automatic webhook processing for payment confirmations
  • Unified reporting across all payment methods
